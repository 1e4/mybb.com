#mybb IRC Channel

If you have an IRC client you can join us on the IRC channel by connecting your client to irc.freenode.net and joining #mybb. You can also connect using the webchat below.

MyBB Staff are voiced (+) in the channel, and are probably around to talk to. The IRC channel is a place for you to talk respectfully with MyBB users and Staff. Official support is provided in the relevant section on the MyBB Community Forums.

You should not disclose vulnerabilities in MyBB within the IRC Channel. Instead, please see the Security Research page for information on how to responsibly disclose vulnerabilities.
